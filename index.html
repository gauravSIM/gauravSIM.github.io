1. INSEAD Collaborative Leadership

Provider: INSEAD Executive Education
Language: English
Website: https://www.insead.edu/executive-education/leadership/collaborative-leadership
Programme Director: Michael Y. Lee, Assistant Professor of Organisational Behaviour

Overview

Explores the shift from top-down hierarchical management to decentralised, empowering leadership models (holacracy, agile, self-management). Combines conceptual frameworks with one-to-one coaching and a personal action plan.

Target Participant

Organisational leaders and experienced managers across all industries managing teams or business units. Also suitable for HR professionals and internal organisational design consultants.

Core Topics

  • Functions and dysfunctions of traditional hierarchies
  • Decentralised organising models (holacracy, agile, self-management)
  • Collaborative leadership in practice: architecture, adult-to-adult communication, meeting facilitation
  • Psychological safety
  • Empowered and agile decision-making
  • Personal collaborative leadership action plan

6. IMD — High Performance Leadership (HPL)

Provider: IMD Business School
Location: Lausanne, Switzerland
Language: English
Website: https://www.imd.org/leadership/hpl/

Overview

One of Europe’s most acclaimed short leadership programmes, running since 2002. The defining characteristic: YOU are the case study, not a company. Over 6 intensive days, participants explore their unique leadership roots and patterns, emotional intelligence, and the psychology of high performance. Director George Kohlrieser is internationally known for applying hostage negotiation principles to leadership — the idea that leaders must avoid becoming “psychological hostages” to fear, pressure, or other people’s agendas.

Target Participant

Open to all levels — from functional managers to C-suite. Particularly valued by executives who have done broad general management programmes and want something deeper and more personal.

Core Topics

  • Leadership self-discovery: roots, patterns, strengths and blind spots
  • Emotional intelligence and authentic connection with teams
  • Focus and mental performance techniques used by world-class athletes and artists
  • Principles of hostage negotiation applied to leadership
  • Creating psychological safety and trust
  • Post-programme coaching (included)

  • Organisational resilience: embedding adaptive capacity into systems and culture

  • Fireside chat: Angela Duckworth on grit and perseverance
